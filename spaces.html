Spaces

Indira is not just an auditorium! We have a variety of rooms that can be rented for one-off events or recurring use. If you'd like to enquire about availability and pricing, please message us on Instagram: instagram.com/indiracinema. The auditorium may be available for special screenings and events; please enquire.

kathak

For years, this magical circular room has played host to twice-weekly Kathak lessons. The room has mirrors on most walls and access to our charming circular balcony. It is perfect for small-group classes, rehearsals, and workshops.

gallery

This sun-bathed space has large windows and mirrors along one wall. It is lovely as an art gallery, or as a space for yoga, dance, or fitness classes.

paradiso

This room, named after the 1988 classic Cinema Paradiso, hosts our projectionist's booth and vintage film projectors. It has access to our circular terrace and our iconic neon ইন্দিরা sign. It is great for private parties and exhibitions.

stalls

This large rectangular room was once the "stalls" section of the auditorium. It is now a versatile space with generous ceilings, its own street entrance, and men's and women's restrooms that are exclusively accessible from this room. It features an attached kitchenette and connects to ample semi-covered service areas. It can be used for large gatherings, performances, exhibitions, and parties.
